# Warm Editorial Light

Use for product stories, teaching posts, demos, and human-centered explanations.

## Palette

- Warm off-white background.
- Deep ink text.
- One warm accent and one cool support color.
- Avoid beige-heavy monotony.

## Layout

- Editorial hierarchy.
- Product scenes and screenshots can be large.
- Use captions and callouts sparingly.
- Let examples carry the slide.

## Visuals

- Phone mockups.
- Browser scenes.
- Before/after journeys.
- Analogy diagrams.
