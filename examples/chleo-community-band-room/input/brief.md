# Chleo Lee Community Band Room Proposal Brief

Build a source-backed HTML pitch deck for Chleo Lee's Singapore community band room idea.

The deck should help her decide how to make the studio real without rushing into lease risk. It needs to explain the market gap, validation plan, property scouting, legality and approval risks, acoustics, fitout, equipment, startup costs, break-even pricing, operating culture, and a 90-day execution plan.

Audience:

- Chleo Lee and close collaborators.
- A junior teammate learning how a source-backed deck workflow should run.
- Potential early partners, teachers, and supporters.

Output:

- A polished mobile-friendly HTML deck.
- Presenter controls, overview mode, and speaker notes.
- Source-backed claims and appendices for equipment, quote collection, and startup cost breakdown.

Design direction:

- Light, professional, expressive.
- Creative Mode influence from `frontend-slides`, with clear visual hierarchy.
- Avoid shallow AI copy, awkward wrapping, unbalanced whitespace, and source/footer collisions.
