# Research Audit

## What Was Challenged

- "There are no affordable rehearsal spaces": rejected. Singapore has budget rooms around S$25 to S$35 per hour.
- "Industrial is the obvious cheap answer": rejected. Industrial space may be cheaper, but use approval and 60:40 restrictions can block the model.
- "S$25k gets a studio": narrowed. S$25k can help with a lean room only if premises are already suitable and isolation expectations are modest.
- "Soundproof panels solve noise": rejected. Acoustic treatment and isolation are different cost categories.
- "Mini debuts can be part of launch": deferred. Public showcases can trigger PE/AEL/SCDF/music-rights checks.

## Remaining Evidence Gaps

- Exact approved use for a real address.
- Actual landlord, MCST or HDB/JTC consent.
- Acoustic leakage measurement for shortlisted premises.
- Contractor quotes for three build tiers.
- Insurance quotes and policy exclusions.
- Teacher demand and pre-sold monthly hours.

## Deck Rule

Where the evidence is site-specific, the slide must say "confirm" or "subject to approval" rather than presenting a blanket answer.
