# Intake Brief

## Deck Goal

Create a source-backed proposal deck for Chleo Lee on how to make an affordable community band room in Singapore real, legally cautious, financially realistic, and emotionally inviting.

## Audience Shift

From: "A communal band room sounds nice, but the path is fuzzy and maybe expensive."

To: "Start with a legally confirmed rehearsal-plus-lessons room, validate demand before heavy capex, and defer public showcases until the licence path is clear."

## Success Criteria

- The deck can be viewed on mobile and desktop as a static HTML proposal.
- It includes Singapore-specific market, legality, acoustic, and cost evidence.
- It shows several launch options with tradeoffs.
- It has source-backed startup cost ranges and explicit assumptions.
- It calls out blind spots instead of hiding them.
- The flow feels like a professional pitch, not a loose research dump.

## Constraints

- Output: static HTML first, publishable on GitHub Pages or similar.
- Style: light theme, polished, presentation-ready, mobile-friendly.
- Accuracy bar: strict for regulations and costs.
- Source policy: broad current research, with official Singapore sources prioritized for legality.
- Presenter style: concise, conversational, confident, and cautious where evidence is conditional.

## Assumptions

- "Community band room" means private band rehearsal, beginner-friendly jamming, freelance music lessons, small workshops, and eventually small showcases.
- Launch should avoid public/ticketed live performances until licensing is confirmed.
- The first owned room is assumed at roughly 500 to 800 sq ft, subject to site approval.
- Commercial lease cash planning uses a typical 3-month security deposit plus first-month rent; actual terms must be negotiated.
- Working capital assumes roughly 3 months of fixed operating costs because occupancy will ramp slowly.

## Open Questions

- Exact site and landlord/MCST rules.
- Whether the intended use will be approved as a music school/commercial school, private studio, event venue, or another use.
- Actual acoustic leakage from shortlisted units.
- Chleo's available startup budget and risk tolerance.
- Whether the first step should be partner pilot or own room.
