# Deck Plan

Date: 2026-06-12
Deck title: Chleo Lee's Community Band Room

## Contract

- deck_job: Singapore business proposal and action plan.
- audience: Chleo Lee and close collaborators deciding whether and how to pursue a community band room.
- presenter: Haoming or Chleo.
- target_next_action: Run the 90-day proof sprint before signing a lease.
- talk_length: 10 to 15 minutes, with appendix for questions.
- output_format: Static HTML deck with presenter controls and mobile scaling.

## Promise

Chleo can make the studio real by validating demand, confirming site permission, quoting the room properly and launching as rehearsal plus lessons before expanding into public showcases.

## Audience Shift

- from: A community band room sounds exciting, but the path is fuzzy, risky and potentially expensive.
- to: The dream is actionable if Chleo treats the first 90 days as evidence gathering, not lease hunting.

## Proof Spine

1. Singapore already has cheap jamming rooms, so trust and teacher-readiness are the wedge.
2. Competitor price bands show where rehearsal, recording and livestream offers sit.
3. Demand should be validated through interviews, pencil-in slots, paid partner-room tests and only later refundable holds.
4. Location and use approval are the main risk gates before rent.
5. Acoustic isolation, fitout, equipment and monthly paid hours decide whether the room is financially workable.
6. The decision is go, partner-pilot, or pause after the proof sprint.

## Design Direction

Chosen direction: warm editorial light with Creative Mode energy, Sakura Chroma color accents and Stencil & Tablet structure. Use fixed 16:9 presenter behavior on desktop and scaled stage on mobile.

Design rules:

- Keep titles as takeaways.
- Align peer cards and tables to a visible grid.
- Use source links in footers for factual slides.
- Keep presenter controls clear of sources and content.
- Use appendices for dense gear and cost detail.

## QA Plan

- Validate slide specs, claim ledger and design contract.
- Run source audit and research critique.
- Use Playwright for desktop and mobile overflow checks.
- Inspect high-risk slides: market, competition, location, legal, equipment, startup costs, revenue, culture and sprint.
- Humanize copy without adding claims.

## Open Questions

- Actual Chleo budget and risk tolerance.
- Preferred neighborhood and acceptable commute.
- Whether a partner-room pilot is available through a friendly studio, school or community space.
- Final use classification for a real address.
- Actual quotes from acoustic, renovation, insurance and equipment vendors.
