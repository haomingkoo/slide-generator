The Heuristic Loop

1

Research. Gather market, property, acoustic, equipment, insurance and legality sources.

2

Ground claims. Convert facts into a claim ledger so slide claims are traceable.

3

Choose the theme. Pick a light, founder-friendly Creative Light direction before full rendering.

4

Render and inspect. Use browser screenshots, mobile checks and presenter controls, not just code review.

5

Repair from critique. Fix language, alignment, wrapping, whitespace, sources, footers and slide flow.

6

Publish. Ship a static HTML deck that Chleo can open on phone or desktop.
