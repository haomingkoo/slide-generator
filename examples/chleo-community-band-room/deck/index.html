AcousticsNoise is the deal-breaker
Critical distinction

Treatment is not isolation.

Treatment

Panels, clouds, bass traps and gobos make the room clearer. They do not stop drums from leaving the room.

Isolation

Mass, seals, doors, decoupling and HVAC silencing control leakage. This is where the cost can jump.

Sources: Sweetwater, Soundzipper, NEA noise guidanceC007-C010

Construction logic

Build the room in layers.

0

Before lease

  • Walk the site with acoustic contractor.
  • Check neighbours, doors, windows, ceiling voids.
  • Check AC, fresh air, exits and power.
1

Openable room

  • Door seals or acoustic door if needed.
  • Wall panels, clouds, bass traps.
  • Drum rug, gobos, storage, lighting.
2

If needed

  • Double wall or decoupled ceiling.
  • Floating or isolated floor works.
  • HVAC silencers and measured test.
