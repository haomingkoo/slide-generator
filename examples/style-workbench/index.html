Pick, mix, preview

Build the slide style before the slides.

Start with a few inspirations. Mix a template, palette and deck logic. Preview the result, then export a prompt the slide-generator can use without guessing your taste.

Source policy

  • Templates: open-source templates are used only when the license and notice are preserved.
  • Palettes: public palette sites are references, not endorsements or copied brand systems.
  • Style systems: external galleries teach artifact shape and QA discipline, not imitation.
  • Motion: frame references guide pacing and composition, not asset reuse.
  • Private decks: user-provided slide exports are pattern references only. No raw private assets are included here.
